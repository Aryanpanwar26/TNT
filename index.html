When initiating a rally-

Take your best heroes when initiating a rally. Adjust your troops so that you can join other rallies too to increase the damage.

When joining a rally-

When joining a rally, your first(left-most side) heroes should be Jessie, Jasser and Seo Yoon. Other 2 heroes doesn't matter.

Arena

So, regarding the Arena, there are some few points that might be beneficial to gain more points:

  • Pay attention to your lineup, you need to select the right lineup for both phases (defensive is while you are away, and the other phase while you do your challenges)
  • Prefered to use infantry at the front line, like Surgery, use lancers at sides or front, and finally marksman at back.
  • Do your challenges at the end of day.
  • I usually spend gems to gain more challenge attempts.
